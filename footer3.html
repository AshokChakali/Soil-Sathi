Real-Time Mill Scheduling

Farmers can view live schedules for nearby mills directly on the platform. This helps them book a specific time slot, eliminating the need to wait in long queues for hours or days. This feature significantly reduces travel time and fuel costs.

✅ **Slot Booking**: Reserve your mill time to guarantee service.

✅ **Queue Tracker**: See the current queue length from your phone.

✅ **Route Optimization**: Get the best route to your scheduled mill.

Receive personalized recommendations for crops and fertilizers based on your specific location and soil type. Our platform provides data-driven insights to help you maximize your yield and improve the health of your crops, leading to better profits.

✅ **Personalized Plans**: Get a tailored farming plan for your land.
